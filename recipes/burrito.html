Description

This yummy black bean burrito is packed with bold flavors. A soft flour tortilla is filled with black beans, fluffy rice, melty cheese, and zesty salsa. Add fresh avocado, sour cream, and crunchy lettuce.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Black Beans
  • Sour Cream
  • Avocado, lettuce, tomato
  • Cheddar
  • Salsa
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook and season meat
  2. Warm and crisp tortilla
  3. Slice and prepare toppings
  4. Build desired tacos and enjoy!
